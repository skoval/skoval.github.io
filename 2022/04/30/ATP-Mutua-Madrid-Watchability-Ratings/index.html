ATP Mutua Madrid Watchability Ratings

April 30, 2022
watchability

It’s the final weeks of the clay court circuit. Madrid will be the next big event on the men’s calendar and the first event of the year to feature Nadal and Djokovic. If Nadal and Djokovic were to meet, it would be a must-see match. But what about the matches in the early rounds? Which are likely to be the most exciting to watch?

Watchability ratings combine the quality and competitiveness of match to give an overall ‘watchability’ score. The technical details can be found here. The tables below shows the most to least watchable ratings for Madrid’s first round matches and the most probable second round matches. So if you can’t catch all the hours of tennis in the first days at the Caja Magica, this is one way to help prioritize your watchlist.
