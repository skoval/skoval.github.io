June 25, 2022
watchability

Even with all the storylines swirling around the 2022 Wimbledon Championships, Serena Williams has managed to top the headlines with her decision to return to SW19 after more than a year from competition. All eyes will be on Williams, the true wildcard of the draw, as she makes her way towards the title. Here are some of the other most watchable contenders for the first week.

Watchability ratings combine the quality and competitiveness of match to give an overall ‘watchability’ score. The technical details can be found here.
