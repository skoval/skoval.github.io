Women's US Open Watchability Ratings

August 27, 2022
watchability

With the 2022 US Open set to be Serena Williams’s farewell event, all attention will be on the GOAT and the coda she ends her career with. If Williams does want to get into the second week, it is going to be a real challenge given the difficulty of her draw. And if the Wilson balls are flying as unusually this year as last, it could be another wild two weeks at Flushing Meadows. Here are some guesses at the matches that could be the toughest and most watchable in the first two rounds.

Watchability ratings combine the quality and competitiveness of match to give an overall ‘watchability’ score. The technical details can be found here.
